🏰 Forts

Ancient fortresses & UNESCO heritage

Bahrain Fort

UNESCO World Heritage Site. Layers of civilisation dating back 4,000 years.

Go at golden hour — the light over the sea is unreal.

Arad Fort

A beautifully restored Portuguese-era fort in Muharraq. Atmospheric at dusk.

Combine with a walk along the Muharraq corniche.

Riffa Fort

Perched on a cliff overlooking Hunanaiya Valley. Far less crowded than Bahrain Fort.

A true hidden gem.

Qalaat Bu Maher

Coastal fort partially submerged at high tide. Accessible by boat from the National Museum.

Ask at the museum about boat trips.

Bahrain National Museum

Brings Bahrain's ancient Dilmun story to life. Starting point for the Bu Maher boat ride.

Spend at least 2 hours here.

🌿 Nature

Desert wonders & natural landmarks

Tree of Life

A 400-year-old mesquite tree alone in the desert. No water source. No explanation.

Best visited at sunset or under a full moon.

Al Bilaj Beach

One of Bahrain's most serene unspoilt beaches. Far from the crowds.

Perfect for a quiet sunrise.

Al Areen Wildlife Park

Bahrain's only wildlife sanctuary. Arabian oryx, gazelles, ostriches roaming freely.

Go in the morning when animals are most active.

🏺 Crafts

Pottery, weaving & traditional arts

Pottery Village — A'ali

The oldest continuously working pottery village in the Gulf.

You can try making your own piece at some workshops.

Bani Jamra Textile Factory

Traditional textile factory where craftsmen weave fabric on ancient wooden looms.

Go on a weekday morning to see the weavers working.

⚡ Adventure & Sports

Thrills, outdoor experiences & activities

Gravity Indoor Skydiving

The closest thing to freefall without jumping out of a plane.

Book in advance — slots fill up fast on weekends.

Bahrain International Circuit

World class karting facilities near the iconic F1 circuit.

Bahrain International Circuit offers karting on race weekends.

🛍 Cafes & Markets

Local favourites & hidden gems

Farmers Market — Hoorat Aali

Vibrant weekly market with fresh local produce, homemade goods, and artisan crafts.

Go early — the best stalls sell out fast.
