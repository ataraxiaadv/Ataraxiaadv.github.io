What type of traveller are you?

8 questions. No right answers. Just the truth about how you actually travel.

The Wanderer The Budget Ninja The Adventurer
Question 1 of 8

01 —

You have 10 days and no plan. What happens first?

A I buy a one-way ticket to somewhere I've never been and figure it out when I land
B I spend a week finding the cheapest flights, best hostels and free things to do
C I look up the best hikes, trails and outdoor experiences and plan around those

02 —

Your ideal place to sleep on a trip is...

A Wherever I end up — a stranger's couch, a last-minute hostel, the back of a car
B A €15-a-night guesthouse that's actually nicer than most hotels
C A tent under the stars — preferably somewhere with no phone signal

03 —

A local offers to take you somewhere "not in any guidebook". You...

A Say yes immediately. This is exactly why you travel
B Ask if it's free first, then say yes immediately
C Ask if there's a trail involved. If yes, you're already putting your boots on

04 —

Your travel budget for two weeks is €600. You feel...

A Fine. Money follows if you stay flexible and say yes to the right things
B Confident. I've already mentally mapped out how to make it work
C Great — most of the best experiences cost nothing anyway

05 —

The moment you feel most alive on a trip is...

A When you're completely lost in a city you've never been to and you don't care
B When you realise you've had an incredible day and spent almost nothing
C Standing at the top of something — a mountain, a cliff, a waterfall — breathless

06 —

What's always in your bag?

A A notebook, a camera, and as little else as possible
B A spreadsheet of free things to do, backup payment cards, and snacks from the local market
C Hiking boots, a water filter, a headtorch, and a tent

07 —

Your friends describe your travel style as...

A "Chaotic but somehow always ends up somewhere incredible"
B "I don't know how they do it for so cheap but I'm always jealous"
C "They come back exhausted, sunburnt, and happier than anyone I've ever seen"

08 —

The trip that changed you most involved...

A Going somewhere completely alone with no plan and finding exactly what you needed
B Realising you can experience the world fully without spending a fortune
C A physical challenge — a summit, a crossing, a dive — that showed you what you're made of
